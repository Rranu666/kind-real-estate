Renter Rights Hub

Built for California's Renters Too

45% of California households rent — yet every real estate platform ignores them. Kind RE is the first platform that genuinely serves renters with real tools, real protections, real transparency.

Lease Plain-Language Analyser
Paste your lease → our AI flags risky clauses in plain English. Know what you're signing before you sign.
⚠ Watch Out
"Tenant waives right to jury trial." — California courts often find these unenforceable, but don't rely on it. Request removal.
⚠ Watch Out
"Early termination fee of 3 months rent." — May be unenforceable. CA law requires landlords to mitigate damages. Consult our legal guide.
✓ Standard
"30-day written notice required to vacate." — Standard for leases under 1 year in California. You're protected.
Deposit Protection Tracker
AB 12 (2024): security deposits now capped at 1 month's rent. Track your deposit, get legal deadline reminders, and document move-in condition with timestamped photos.
AB 12 Quick Facts
Max deposit: 1 month's rent (as of July 2024) · Return deadline: 21 days after move-out · Deductions for normal wear & tear: ILLEGAL
Know Your Eviction Rights
AB 1482 protects millions of California tenants from no-fault eviction. Know if you're protected — and what your landlord must prove to evict you.
✓ Just Cause Required (After 12 months)
Non-payment · Lease violation · Owner move-in (with relocation pay) · Substantial renovation (with relocation pay)
✗ NOT Allowed (AB 1482 covered units)
Evicting because you want to raise rent · Retaliation for reporting habitability issues · Discrimination of any kind
💡 Relocation Assistance
For no-fault evictions, you are owed 1 month's rent as relocation assistance — many tenants don't claim this.
